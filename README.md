# Food Journal

A little diary of what you eat.

Food Journal is a calm, private place to remember the meals and drinks of your day.
Snap a photo, jot a few notes, give it a rating, and build up a scrollable diary of everything you've eaten over time.

Live at **[ezragillooly.github.io/food-journal](https://ezragillooly.github.io/food-journal/)**.

## What it's for

Most food apps want to count your calories or turn eating into a chore.
This one doesn't.
It's just a pretty, personal journal - the point is to notice and remember what you ate, not to track or optimize anything.

It works beautifully on a phone, so logging a meal takes only a few seconds while you're still at the table.

## What you can do

- **Log a meal or drink** with a photo, a name, and the time you had it.
- **Rate it 1-10** so you remember whether it was worth it.
- **Say whether you made it or bought it**, and tag where you had it.
- **Add notes and the recipe or ingredients**, so you can recreate the good ones.
- **Sort it into a category** - breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, or drink.
- **Browse your journal** grouped by day, newest first, each entry photo-forward.
- **Find things fast** by searching a name or filtering by category and made-vs-bought.
- **Edit or delete** any entry whenever you like.
- **Pick your look** from a few soft, hand-picked themes.

## Your journal is yours

Every account is completely private.
You only ever see your own entries and photos - no feeds, no followers, no sharing.
Sign up with an email and a password, and your journal syncs across your phone and laptop.

## The look

Soft, warm, and minimal - inspired by a cottagecore palette of creams and dusty roses, with an elegant serif for the little details.
If it's not quite your taste, there are a few other themes to choose from in settings.
